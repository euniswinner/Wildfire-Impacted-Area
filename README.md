**Exploring the Practical Impact of Geography**
I started this project with a fundamental quetion:"How can geography be applied to solve real-world problem?" 
I noticed that many goegraphy graduates pursue careers in public agencies and the **insurance industry**.
Wondering why insurance companies highly value geography, I realized that spatial and GIS are essential
for risk mapping-specifically for assessing and prediciting natural diasters driven by climate change. 
**Analyzing California Wildfire Data:**
TO see this in action, I chose to work with real-world wildfire data from California during my Coursera specialization. 
By overlapping county boundaries with wildfire zones, I gained hands-on experience in how institutions quantify environmental risks
and make data-driven decisions using ArcGIS Pro 3.7.
